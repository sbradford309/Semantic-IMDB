    Did You Know?

    Trivia

    The character of Wayulia Tagge-Simoni, briefly seen during the Canto Bight sequence, is inspired by Eugène Spaak's hologram head in Martin Villeneuve's sci-fi romance Mars et Avril (2012). Martin is the younger brother of Blade Runner 2049 (2017) director Denis Villeneuve and gave a TED Talk at TED2013 (which Lucasfilm Exec Kathleen Kennedy attended) in which he explains how he came up with this original idea. See TEDTalks: Martin Villeneuve: How I made an impossible film (2013). Spaak's head is actually a hologram, with all of his ideas, memories and thoughts stored electronically. See more »

    Goofs

    The First Order flagship was firing at the Raddus. The rounds that were fired were traveling in a curved line towards the Raddus, and while energy bolts being fired in space and outside of a gravity field would only travel in a straight line, missiles can be target-programmed to adjust their trajectory.
